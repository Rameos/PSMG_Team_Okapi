Insgesamt ca. 90 sek, also 30 sek für jeden Abschnitt

Teil 1:

Musik: Ruhig, einführend, nur wenig Dissonanz

Terminal Text: "My name is Roland Kozani. I am the fifth quarterly scientist of the 'Salomon Research Center' and a member of the P.A.R.I.A.-Project. I am the sole researcher in this facility, and I am investigating the origins and properties of - so far - unclassified life forms. One of my predecessors named these creatures the 'Scyth'. We know practically nothing about them."

Film: Gänge, Schlafraum, Labor, blubbernde Chemikalien, evtl. auch Scyth (idle, im Inkubator)

Übergang: Licht aus, Impact mit Ausklang

Teil 2:

Musik: Takt-Wechsel, schnelleres Tempo, mehr Dissonanz

Terminal Text: "They got out. At least two of them. And they seem to be attacking me." "I'm going to have to go down to the generator to get the power on again." "But this outpost is built like a labyrinth. I'm running in circles. And I think I heard more scyth."

Film: Labor mit Notstrom, Gänge, Generator?

Übergang: Möglichst Fließend

Teil 3:

Musik: Dissonante Klänge ohne erkennbaren Rhytmus oder Melodie; Herzschlag, Atmen.

Terminal Text: "How many of those things are down here?" "Why are the doors and the lights working but not the cages?" "We don't even know what they eat. IF they eat." "This simply doesn't make any sense." "I know this sounds crazy, but I think somebody else down here." "WHERE ARE YOU? SHOW YOURSELF!"

Film: Rückelnde Kamera, Gänge mit Blut

Ende: Schrei, Scyth im Angriff-Modus, Blackout.
